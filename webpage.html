About

Founded in 1981 by vasavi academy of Education,vasavi college of engineering represents a rich tradition of excellence in technology-based education. A premier institution among the affliates of osmania university,vasavi college institution owes its vision to sri pendikanti subbaiah,a veteran statesman of Independant India.
